Educational Visit

Finland possesses a unique education system, one of the best in the world. We have the best system because we see education not merely in numbers but instead we aim to build a valuable full develop human resources. Grades are not the priority but how to create an environment where student find their motivation to study things that they love.

We arrange educational visit to Finland. We have arranged visits to Finnish schools and arranged seminars or workshop on education in Finland and in Indonesia.

Our educational visits are for individual companies, teachers, school owners and Indonesian government bodies whom are seriously want to learn further about Finnish education system.

Summer Experience

Our Summer camp is a cultural and education experience for young students. We will teach them to be more independent, experiencing a real Finnish Summer activities and learning new skills together with the local children.

Winter Experience

We will learn basic Winter survival skills and experiencing Finland's Winter tradition e.g sauna, ice swimming. More often we arrange the trip in the North of Finland, Lapland.

Bicycle Tour

We arrange bicycle trip around Finnish villages and islands. The tour will be at least for 2 days, including staying over in a village house.
